About

Built by a founder,
for founders

ConversionCRM exists because the most painful problem in SaaS — converting free users to paid — still doesn't have a simple, prebuilt solution. Until now.

ConversionCRM is free trial conversion software for SaaS — it tracks in-product behavior with one script tag, scores each free user 0–100 across six engagement layers, and sends 8 automated behavior-triggered emails that move signups to paid under your sender name. See how it works →

Why ConversionCRM exists

Most SaaS products lose 88–95% of their free signups before they ever pay. The users weren't bad leads — they just never got the right nudge at the right moment. They drifted away while the product sat silent.

The existing tools that solve this either require weeks of setup (Customer.io, HubSpot), cost thousands per month (Intercom, Pendo), or only do half the job (Mailchimp handles broadcast, not behavior). A founder building a SaaS on the weekend can't use any of them to get lifecycle emails running this week.

ConversionCRM is the missing tool: one script tag, and the whole signup-to-paid engine runs — scoring, lifecycle stages, and 8 behavior-triggered emails — without any journey design, segment configuration, or data pipeline work.

About the founder

S

Saarthi

Founder, ConversionCRM

I built ConversionCRM after watching product after product struggle with the same problem: plenty of signups, not enough conversions. The gap wasn't the product itself — it was the silence between sign-up and upgrade. Nobody was reaching users at the right moment with the right message.

I wanted a tool that treated every free user as an individual: score their engagement from their actual behavior, know what lifecycle stage they're in, and send them the email that makes sense right now — not on day 3 because someone set a timer.

ConversionCRM is that tool. It's built for founders and small teams who need something working this week, not after a month of integration work.
