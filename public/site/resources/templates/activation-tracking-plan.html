What is an activation tracking plan?

It is a one page map of what you measure to convert trials: your single activation event, the six behavior layers you score, and the email each signal triggers. Filling it in forces the decisions that make a behavior triggered system work, before you write a line of code.

Fill this in for your own product before you build anything. The example row shows the shape. Copy it into a sheet, or just use it as the spec when you set up tracking.

Your activation event

FieldYour answerExample
Activation event name[ your event ]first_project_created
Why it predicts retention[ your reason ]Converters do it in week one, churned trials do not
Target window[ hours or days ]Within 72 hours of signup

Your six scoring inputs

LayerThe signal in your productExample
Recency[ last active ]Days since last session
Frequency[ return pattern ]Sessions in last 7 days
Depth[ breadth of use ]Distinct features used
Key feature[ activation event ]Reached first_project_created
Time spent[ usage ]Total active minutes
Buying intent[ intent signals ]Pricing visits, upgrade clicks, limit hits

Your email triggers

ConditionEmail to sendExample timing
[ score or behavior ][ email ]Score 70 plus, send upgrade offer
[ inactivity ][ email ]5 days quiet, send win back
[ intent ][ email ]Pricing visit, send urgency

Once this is filled in, the build is mechanical. ConversionCRM scores all six layers automatically and fires the emails on these conditions, so the plan above becomes the configuration rather than a spec you hand to engineering.
