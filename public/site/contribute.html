Write for ConversionCRM

We publish practical, honest, well sourced writing for SaaS founders and product teams. If that is you, we would love your pitch.

Try Beta See how it works
Who should write for ConversionCRM?

Founders, product managers, growth people, and developers who have actually shipped trial conversion work and can write about it specifically. We accept practical, sourced articles on activation, lifecycle email, scoring, onboarding, and product led growth, and you keep your byline and a link.

What we are looking for

Writing that helps a SaaS founder do something on Monday. Concrete tactics, real examples, and numbers that trace to a real source. If your piece could only have been written by someone who has done the work, it is a fit.

Topics we accept

  • Trial to paid conversion and free to paid benchmarks
  • User activation and finding the aha moment
  • Lifecycle and behavior triggered email
  • Engagement scoring and lifecycle stages
  • Onboarding and time to value
  • Product led growth and product led sales

The basics

  • Original writing, not published anywhere else.
  • Roughly 1,200 to 2,000 words, structured with clear headings.
  • Real sources for every statistic, linked.
  • Plain, specific voice. No filler, and in keeping with our house style, no dashes.
  • You keep your byline and a short bio with one link.

How to pitch

Send a two line pitch with your proposed title, the one idea it delivers, and a link to something you have written. Email ceo.conversioncrm@gmail.com with the subject Guest post pitch. We read every one and reply if it is a fit.

Send a pitch

Not sure what to write? Browse the blog and the resource library to see the depth and voice we are after, then pitch the gap you can fill.
